## Phase Details

### Phase 1: FAQ Accuracy
**Goal**: Visitors reading FAQ pages encounter technically accurate, credible answers that build trust with expert buyers
**Depends on**: Nothing (first phase)
**Requirements**: FAQ-01, FAQ-02, FAQ-03, FAQ-04, FAQ-05, FAQ-06, FAQ-07
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A PPF-knowledgeable visitor reads the PPF FAQ and finds no factually incorrect claims about film thickness, self-healing, or warranty
  2. A ceramic coating FAQ reader sees longevity claims and maintenance advice consistent with SunTek/Solar Gard published specs
  3. A QLD resident reads the Automotive Tinting FAQ and finds the legal VLT limits cited correctly for front and rear windows
  4. Residential and Commercial Tinting FAQ answers cite verifiable energy/solar performance figures with no contradictions
  5. All corrected answers have citation comments in source code and use consistent product terminology
**Plans**: TBD

### Phase 2: Reviews Integration
**Goal**: Site visitors see real customer ratings on arrival, building immediate trust and enabling Google rich result star display
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: REV-01, REV-02, REV-03, REV-04
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A first-time visitor to the homepage sees an aggregate star rating and review count without scrolling below the fold
  2. At least 3 real customer review snippets are visible on the homepage or a service page
  3. AggregateRating JSON-LD is present in page source so Google can display stars in search results
  4. A customer who just received their car back can find and click a "Leave a Google Review" link from the site
**Plans**: TBD

### Phase 3: Quote Form Delivery
**Goal**: Every quote request submitted via the form reaches the business and the customer receives confirmation
**Depends on**: Phase 1
**Requirements**: FORM-01, FORM-02, FORM-03
**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
  1. A customer submits the quote form and the business receives an email with all submitted details within 60 seconds
  2. The submitting customer receives an auto-reply confirmation email acknowledging their enquiry
  3. After submission the UI shows a clear success message; if the submission fails the UI shows an error and does not silently drop the enquiry
